Workshops

Travels

In 2023 I came over one of my fears, namely solo travelling. As I am following many crochet and yarn artist all around the world, in March a post crossed my path from Rita Cavallaro where she showed one of her masterpiece and referred to an event, where she is going to show it.
30 minutes later I was checking Google Maps, all the cheap flights and the event itself, what she was referring to: Yarnbombing Barzano. Long story short: after seeing all the breahtaking pieces and the festival itself, a few hours later all was settled for my journey in June to Barzano. Without hesitation and any questionmarks I just knew, I have to be there. So I was.

I could not be happier that I fought through my anxiety, not just because I've met super cute people, wonderful artists and generally was a great adventure - but because there I've met an amazing crafty women (Migliarino Blue)from Vienna! Since then we keep in touch, I can not adore her dedication and creativity enough to zero waste, that she introduced me to the world of crafty markets and generally, her friendship.
