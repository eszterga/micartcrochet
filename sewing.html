New addiction: sewing. Since forever I wished to learn sewing, but there was always a BUT. Time, budget, space, the scarcity that I will fall into this rabbithole as well.

Apparently yes, I did.

Since recycling and environmental consciousness are important to me, I started with upcycling old clothes. I had a lot of old clothes that I didn't wear anymore. So I started cutting them and sewing them together to make new clothes (Rather experimental pieces til now). Beside the joy of creating something new, my main purpose was to combine crocheting and sewing to have my very own designs. In progress.
